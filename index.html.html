A Letter to My Love

Published on September 29, 2024

in the vastness of this swirling universe,
you are my brightest star,
twinkling (just for me)
with that sweet smile
that makes my heart dance
like leaves in a gentle breeze.

“i carry your heart with me(i carry it in my heart)”
— your love is the softest whisper,
the laughter of springtime,
the gentle rain that kisses the earth,
and every moment spent with you
is a treasure, a soft melody,
a beautiful poem
that plays just for us.

each day, i find a new reason
to love you more (and more)
like how your eyes sparkle
when you laugh,
how you make even the clouds
look like fluffy cotton candy
with your happiness.
your joy is a kaleidoscope,
a dance of colors
that fills my heart
with warm sunshine.

(let’s play pretend)
we can be explorers
on a magical journey,
where dreams bloom
like wildflowers in spring,
and every heartbeat
is a song that sings
the words of our love.

so here’s a little poem,
just for you:

when i look at you,
i see the universe—
stars sparkling,
the moon sighing,
and all the wishes
i’ve ever made
floating softly
on the wings of your laughter.

you make my world brighter,
and in every smile you give,
i see endless possibilities.
let’s dance under the stars,
chasing the moonbeams,
and wrap ourselves
in the warmth of our love.

always remember:
you are my favorite hello,
and my hardest goodbye.
with you, every day is a dream,
a beautiful adventure,
and i can’t wait
to create more memories
together.

with all my love,
(always and forever)
your playful poet
